How can we help the environment?

About 75% of a person's waste is recycable, but only 21.4% is actually recycled!

When these items enter a landfill, they sit there for many years.

Plastic items, which can often be recycled, can take 1,000 years to decompose!

The plastic that you don't recycle will be on this planet for the next 10 generations.

If we were to bring our recycling rates to 75 percent, it would be the equivalent of removing about 55 million cars from the road each year. This would reduce around 253,000,000,000 metric tons of carbon dioxide released per year!
